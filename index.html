Who We Are

A leader in the pharmaceutical marketplace since 1994, ERS helps clients to realize big picture goals, while optimizing sales training opportunities, and maximizing marketing reach with an effective promotional program.

With over two decades of experience, the Sales Training division of ERS has been exceeding client expectations with its unique blend of extensive therapeutic knowledge, unmatched creativity, and account management expertise.
The Medical Communications division of ERS offers the full range of pre/post-launch promotional programs and communication services for pharmaceutical clients. ERS offers an advantage to clients by employing expert account management teams who are skilled in combining scientific proficiency, marketing know-how and skillful client servicing expertise.
